Step 0 · Environment

Prepare or reset the live baseline

Reuse a seeded deployment if available, or perform a fresh reset before presenting the full thesis walkthrough.

Use the hero actions for launch and refresh. This card is reserved for hard reset before a clean demo run.

Step 1 · Forward proof path

Lock assets on Bank A and prove them on Bank B

Open the route, commit the packet, import the finalized Bank A header on Bank B, then verify the storage proof to mint the voucher exactly once.

Step 2 · Lending actions

Turn the voucher into usable collateral

After proof success, the lending pool can accept the voucher, issue credit, track debt, and later release collateral after repayment.

Step 3 · Reverse unlock

Burn on Bank B and release value back on Bank A

The return path mirrors the forward proof path: write the reverse packet, import Bank B trust into Bank A, then verify proof to unlock escrow.

Step 4 · Safety and recovery

Stress the system and make recovery visible

Trigger freeze with conflicting evidence, recover with a newer trust anchor, and keep replay and timeout behavior transparent for the audience.
